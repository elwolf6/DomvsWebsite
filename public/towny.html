How do I found a town?

In order to create a Town you need $250, to check your balance do /money. You get money from killing mobs or by selling items at Elysium. Go to the land that you want to found your town in and do /t new {town name} for {town name} make sure you don't put spaces(use underscores) and no special characters. To expand your town, go to a chunk that you want to claim (F3+G to see the chunk borders) and do /t claim.

Mayors and Assistants can claim land for a town. To add a player to your town do /t add {player name} after that, the player you invited has to do the command /accept {town name}, only Mayors and Assistants can add members. More information such as permissions can be found on the Towny Wiki or do /t ?.

How do I found a nation?

In order to create a Nation you need $1000 in your town bank, to check your town's balance do /t. You get money from killing mobs or by sellings items at Elysium, you deposit money in your towns bank with /t deposit {amount}.

To found the nation do /n new {name} make sure you don't put spaces (uses underscores) and no special characters. To invite another town to your nation do /n add {town name} the town you invited has to do the command /n accept {nation name}. To invite another nation to be an ally do /n ally add {nation name} they have to accept the request.

To remove a nation as an ally do /n ally remove {nation name}. To add a nation as an enemy do /n enemy add {nation name}. To remove a nation as an enemy do /n enemy remove {nation name}. More information such as permissions can be found on the Towny Wiki or do /t ?.

Flag Wars

Flag war is very simple war mechanic but we have added a rule for iniating it. The town you are attacking must have atleast 3 people online in the town. (This is already set in the plugin)

Before you can start claiming the plot you need to have the town you want to take a plot from placed in the enemy status. You also need an oak fence. As this is needed for initiating the flag war.

To start a flag war you have to place the fence at the edge of the plot you want to conquer. This wil start the war. The attacking side has to defend the flag until the timer expires while the defending side has to demolish the fence with wool ontop of it. If the attacker wins, then the attacking side gets the chunk.

Siege Wars

The attacking side has to place down a colored banner next to the town (in the wilderness next to it) it wants to conquer. This action costs money to do. The banner spawns a 16 block radius zone where both sides have to compete in. Siege war lasts way longer. The period lasts 3 Days. In those 3 days both teams have to compete for points.

The team who has the most points in the end conquers or keeps the town. Whenever this happens a spoil of war bonus in the form of money is given to the winning side.

If the winning side is the attackers then they get 3 options. Sack the whole town of money with empties the town bank and annexing it to the nation. Or just annexation to the nation. Or both.
