Towny Tutorial

Domvs Minecraft uses Towny, here are some of the basics of Towny.

Founding a Town

In order to create a Town you need $250, to check your balance do /money. You get money from killing mobs or by selling items at Elysium. Go to the land that you want to found your town in and do /t new {town name} for {town name} make sure you don't put spaces(use underscores) and no special characters. To expand your town, go to a chunk that you want to claim (F3+G to see the chunk borders) and do /t claim. Only Mayors and Assistants can claim land for a town. To add a player to your town do /t add {player name} after that, the player you invited has to do the command /accept {town name}, only Mayors and Assistants can add members. More information such as permissions can be found on the Towny Wiki or do /t ?.

Founding a Nation

In order to create a Nation you need $1000 in your town bank, to check your town's balance do /t. You get money from killing mobs or by sellings items at Elysium, you deposit money in your towns bank with /t deposit {amount}. To found the nation do /n new {name} make sure you don't put spaces (uses underscores) and no special characters. To invite another town to your nation do /n add {town name} the town you invited has to do the command /n accept {nation name}. To invite another nation to be an ally do /n ally add {nation name} they have to accept the request. To remove a nation as an ally do /n ally remove {nation name}. To add a nation as an enemy do /n enemy add {nation name}. To remove a nation as an enemy do /n enemy remove {nation name}. More information such as permissions can be found on the Towny Wiki or do /t ?.
